About the Creator

I am a 3-year college student at Seattle University where I am pursuing a degree in computer science I created this website for my web design class and I thought it was a good opportunity to do a project that was interesting to me. I was tired of making programs that were not interesting and so I made this website. I have liked music and I always wanted a site where I could make simple music just for fun and that was the goal of this website. I have not completly finished it to my liking so I hope to make more adjustments in the future.

Why Create this Site?

This site was made for people who have an interest in music and it was made to help them learn about music making in a simple and fun manner. I have some simple resources for those newer to music to read and get a better understanding of music. I also made it very simple, just press a key to make a sound, this makes it easier to learn and an easy to pick up site.

Future of this Site

I want to make it so that the user can have accsess to more sounds and more keys. I won't add too much to confuse the user, but to add some other popular instruments like violin. I also want to add a record button so people can record little snippits of music that they have made.
